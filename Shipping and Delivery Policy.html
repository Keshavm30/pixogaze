Shipping and Delivery Policy - Pixogaze

1. Delivery of Digital Products

As a photography service provider, Pixogaze delivers final products (images, videos, etc.) in a digital format:

  • Delivery Method: Completed projects will be delivered electronically via email, cloud storage links (e.g., Google Drive, Dropbox), or a private client portal on our website.
  • Delivery Timeline: We aim to deliver the final edited images within 3-7 business days after the photoshoot, unless otherwise specified in the contract or booking confirmation. In case of video shoots, the timeline may extend depending on the complexity of the project.
  • Rush Delivery: If you need expedited delivery, please contact us in advance. Additional fees may apply for rush orders.

2. Physical Deliverables (If Applicable)

In rare cases where Pixogaze offers physical products such as printed photos, albums, or merchandise:

  • Shipping Method: Physical products will be shipped via a reputable courier service. Shipping fees will be included in the final invoice, unless otherwise agreed.
  • Shipping Locations: We ship to addresses within India. If you require international shipping, please contact us for details and availability.
  • Shipping Timeframe: Once shipped, physical products are expected to arrive within 7-14 business days, depending on your location. Delays due to holidays or unforeseen circumstances may affect delivery times.
  • Tracking: A tracking number will be provided once your order is dispatched. You can use this to monitor the status of your delivery.

3. Delivery Issues

  • Late Delivery: If the delivery of digital products exceeds the expected timeline, please contact us immediately at pixogaze@gmail.com or call +91 95945 18106. We will ensure prompt resolution of any delays.
  • Lost or Damaged Shipments: For physical products, if your order is lost or damaged during shipping, we will work with the courier to resolve the issue. Please report such incidents within 5 days of receiving the damaged product.

4. No Shipping Charges for Digital Products

Since Pixogaze primarily delivers digital products, there are no shipping charges for these services. Physical products, if requested, will have separate shipping fees that will be mentioned at the time of purchase.

5. International Delivery

For clients outside India who request physical products, shipping availability and charges will vary depending on the destination. Please contact us for detailed international shipping information.
